Senior Treasury Analyst

Treasury Advisory
Full-time

We're looking for an experienced treasury analyst to join our advisory team. You'll work directly with clients to optimize their cash flow management, banking relationships, and risk management strategies.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of experience in treasury, corporate finance, or banking
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and client management abilities
  • Knowledge of treasury management systems and banking platforms
  • CA/CFA/MBA preferred

Risk Management Consultant

Risk Advisory
Full-time

Join our risk management team to help clients develop and implement comprehensive risk management strategies. Focus on forex, interest rate, and operational risk management.

Requirements:

  • 4+ years of experience in financial risk management
  • Strong knowledge of derivatives and hedging strategies
  • Experience with risk management frameworks and regulations
  • Proficiency in risk management tools and software
  • FRM/CFA certification preferred

Business Development Manager

Sales & Marketing
Full-time

Drive business growth by identifying new client opportunities and building relationships with potential customers. You'll work closely with our advisory teams to develop tailored solutions.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years of B2B sales experience, preferably in financial services
  • Strong networking and relationship-building skills
  • Understanding of treasury and corporate finance
  • Proven track record of meeting sales targets
  • MBA or relevant business degree preferred
